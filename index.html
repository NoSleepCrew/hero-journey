THE LEGENDARY MINDSET

  • Progress matters more than perfection.
  • Mistakes happen.
  • One mistake does not erase the good choices that came before it, and it does not prevent good choices afterward.
  • What matters most is what you choose to do next.
  • Heroes are not expected to be perfect. Heroes are expected to keep moving forward.
